Baked Feta and Riccota Pasta

This dish is a play on a popular recipe. The baked feta pasta became a viral sensation at the height of covid, but this recipe adds another rich cheese to the mix with ricotta.This creamy pasta dish is simple, quick, and playful in its flavor.

image courtsey of feelgoodfoodie

Here's what you'll need to get started

  • 1/2pd of your choice of pasta
  • 16oz of Feta in block
  • 4oz of Ricotta
  • 2 packages of cherry tomatoes
  • half a red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • basil
  • garlic powder
  • olive oil

Here's to put it all together

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees
  2. Start with a clean medium deep baking dish (this is where everything with end up)
  3. Add both packages of tomatoes to the dish after washing
  4. Cut half an onion to your desired size and add to dish
  5. Cut two cloves of garlic to your desired size and add to dish
  6. Season the vegetables with basil and garlic powder to your liking
  7. Coat the vegetables with olive oil and mix the seasoning and oil with a spoon until everything is coated
  8. Take the 16oz of feta block and place it on top of your vegetables
  9. Take two tablespoons of ricotta, one on either side of the feta (approximately 4oz)
  10. Coat the cheeses with olive oil and season to taste
  11. Put in the oven for 10 minutes
  12. After 10 minutes have passed take a large pot and fill with water, while you are cooking the pasta make sure to set another timer for 10 minutes for the sauce in the oven
  13. Boil water and add salt and olive oil to water if desired
  14. Once water is at a boil add pasta
  15. Follow the direction for the pasta you choose
  16. Once pasta has been drained and sauce dish has finished baking remove from oven and add pasta
  17. Let everything cool and enjoy!
