Tomato Soup

A perfect warm and savory dish for a cold or gloomy day.

image courtesy of Natasha's Kitchen

Here's what you'll need to get started

  • 2 packages of cherry tomatoes
  • half an onion
  • half a bag of baby carrots
  • paleo seasoning, garlic seasoning, salt and pepper
  • vegetable stock

Here's to put it all together

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Put tomatoes, carrots, and separated onion on baking sheet
  3. Bake for 25 minutes
  4. Put hot vegetables in glass dish and add vegetable stock as you use a immersion blender to blend down into soup
