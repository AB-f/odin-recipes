Caprese Salad

This is a simple and refreshing salad that works great as an appetizer.

image courtesy of loveandlemons

Here's what you'll need to get started

  • 16oz of fresh mozzarella
  • 2 packages of cherry tomatoes
  • fresh basil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Here's to put it all together

  1. Slice the mozzarella into 2 inch pieces
  2. Take the fresh basil and put it on top of the mozzarella
  3. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half
  4. Place the tomatoes on top of the basil
  5. Drizzle oil and balsamic vinegar to taste
  6. Finish with salt and pepper if desired
