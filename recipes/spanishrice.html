Spanish Rice

This is a simple and refreshing salad that works great as an appetizer.

image courtesy of simply recipes

Here's what you'll need to get started

  • day old rice
  • 1 carrot
  • half a onion
  • 16oz vegetable stock
  • olive oil
  • mexican seasoning

Here's to put it all together

  1. Chop carrots and onion add to pan with olive oil
  2. Cook vegetables until carrots and onions are soft
  3. Add vegetable stock to veggies
  4. While everything is simmering add one tablespoon of tomato paste and mexican seasoning
  5. Add day old rice
  6. Simmer and mix rice until liquid is absorbed
  7. Finish with salt and pepper if desired
